# Tola — Design Language

> **Concept: "Tola grows with you."**
> Not a cold tracker that audits you — a living thing you tend. Warm, patient,
> encouraging. Every choice should feel made by a person who cares whether you
> showed up today, not generated by a lab.

## Direction: Organic & Human

Earthy, warm, body-positive. Soft shapes, natural tones, growth as the metaphor.
Voice: **warm mentor** — kind, steady, encouraging. "You showed up today. That's
the part that counts." Never clinical, never shaming.

## Typography

- **Display** — Fraunces (soft characterful serif). Greetings, screen titles,
  hero numbers (ring kcal, streak). Inline: `fontFamily: 'Fraunces_700Bold'` /
  `'Fraunces_600SemiBold'`.
- **Body** — Inter. (`Inter_400Regular` / `500Medium` / `600SemiBold`.)
- Counters always `fontVariant: ['tabular-nums']`.
- Fonts loaded in `app/_layout.tsx` via `@expo-google-fonts`.

## Signature motif: the Sprout (`src/components/Sprout.tsx`)

A plant that **grows with your streak** — a seed in soil at 0, a stem + a leaf
pair every ~3 days, flowering at 30. Lives on Home (header) and Progress (streak
card). Your progress is _alive_. This is the thing people screenshot.

## Principles

- **Warmth in every pixel** — warm darks, soft shadows, rounded continuous curves.
- **Rhythm over uniformity** — not every card equal; let the hero (ring, sprout)
  breathe; quiet the supporting info.
- **Emotional choreography** — celebrate wins (flowering, "nourished ✓"), never
  punish a miss ("a fresh start", not red).
- **Motion** (next) — spring fills, a ring that breathes, gentle entrances; haptics
  on log actions. Reanimated is installed.
